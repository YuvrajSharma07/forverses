Marketplace Submission Guidelines

Marketplace publishes unsolicited submissions of poetry and visual art. We are currently a digital journal and a non paying market. With your support we hope to pay our contributors soon.
Please adhere to the following guidelines to submit an entry:

  1. We accept submissions monthly in a window between the 1st and 20th of the month. Our unsolicited submissions are themeless. If we are accepting only themed submissions in the current month, you will find the information below.
  2. We currently accept poetry (upto 60 lines and in English or Hindi), visual art - photography, illustrations and cartoons and comics.
  3. Please upload only pdf, docx, jpeg or png formats.
  4. A maximum of one submission is allowed per person each month. Please title all the submissions, regardless of the category you submit to.
  5. We prefer previously unpublished work but are happy to publish work which has appeared elsewhere before. Please mention the same in the body of the email.
