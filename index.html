About Us

Forbidden Verses is an organisation built to provide and promote the local artists around us. Our aim is to create a judgement free environment and let the voices of the people speak for themselves. We wish to create space for art, art that can empower as well as heal. Forbidden Verses is our little part to bring art to everyone else. This community started in October 2018 with a little open mic that has evolved to be so much more. Through our events we aim to give a space to artists to truly express themselves without any conditions and try to make art more accessible for all.

There are poems that can change the world, paintings that build new ones, photography that captures both the ugly and the beautiful and music which sets this change into motion, and our aim is to bring these glorious artforms under one roof. Our community is ever growing with new art forms being experimented each event and our audiences come not only to listen, but to become parts of little stories themselves. We also believe current social situations can never be divorced from the art we create, hence, we provide a safe space for these difficult dialogues to actually be facilitated. We are open to collaborations with any organisation that aims at making the skyline of Delhi and beyond, happier with art, expression and any civic engagement.
